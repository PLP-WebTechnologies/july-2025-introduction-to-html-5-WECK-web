About This Page

This page demonstrates how to use HTML5 semantic elements, improve accessibility for all users, and follow basic SEO guidelines for better discoverability.

Accessibility Principles

Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility ensures that your website can be used by people with disabilities, including those who rely on screen readers or keyboard navigation.

Best Practices

  • Use proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3).
  • Provide descriptive link text.
  • Include alt attributes for images.
  • Use ARIA attributes when necessary to enhance understanding for assistive technologies.

SEO Best Practices

Why SEO Is Important

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps search engines understand your content, improving your site's visibility in search results.

SEO Tips

  1. Use relevant keywords naturally in your content.
  2. Write descriptive meta tags (title, description).
  3. Ensure mobile-friendly design with a responsive layout.
  4. Structure your HTML with semantic tags for better crawling.
